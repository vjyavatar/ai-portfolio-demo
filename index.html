📈 How It Works

Enter any stock ticker — like AAPL, MSFT, RELIANCE.NS, or HDFCBANK.NS — and Celesys AI pulls live market data from multiple sources including Yahoo Finance, Google Finance, and NSE India. The platform runs a 20-factor quantitative scoring model evaluating valuation (P/E, P/B, PEG, EV/EBITDA), profitability (ROE, margins), financial health (debt-to-equity, current ratio, free cash flow), and momentum (52-week position, SMA/EMA crossovers). The result is a deterministic buy/sell verdict with a conviction score and full explanation.

📋 What You Get in Every Report

Each report includes real-time stock price, intrinsic value calculations using the Benjamin Graham Number, DCF growth model, Peter Lynch fair value (PEG-based), and earnings yield vs Treasury bond comparison. You also receive AI-generated entry and exit price levels with buy zones, accumulate levels, profit targets, and stop-loss prices computed from EMA crossovers, Fibonacci retracements, and 52-week range analysis. Visual charts cover valuation, financial health, risk profile, 6-month price trends, and margin benchmarks.

🤖 Deep AI Analysis & Earnings Intelligence

Beyond numbers, Celesys AI generates comprehensive research powered by advanced AI — including investment thesis, quarterly fundamentals with QoQ and YoY growth trends, management tone assessment with CEO/CFO confidence scoring, institutional and mutual fund holdings, upcoming catalysts and timeline forecasts, and bull/bear/base case price predictions for 12 months. Every section ends with a plain-English inference explaining what the data means for a regular investor.
